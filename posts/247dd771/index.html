\[ ed=1+k(p^2+p+1)(q^2+q+1) \]

\[ ed=1+k(n^2-n+1+(n+1)(p+q)+(p+q)^2) \]

\[ 0\equiv 1+k(n^2-n+1+(n+1)(p+q)+(p+q)^2)\ (mod\ e) \]

令x=k，y=p+q，即可进行二元coppersmith
